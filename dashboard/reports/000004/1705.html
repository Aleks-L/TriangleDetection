Foot Traffic vs. Unique Store Visits

Foot traffic is all people seen in and around your SmartRack, as measured by wifi devices the SmartRack sees. Think of these numbers as your potential customers.

Unique Store Visits is customers that stayed for more than 30 seconds and came within ~20' of the SmartRack. Employees (wifi devices the SmartRack sees every day) are filtered out.
